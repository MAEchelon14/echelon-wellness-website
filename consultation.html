Common Questions Before Scheduling

Is there really no cost or obligation?

Absolutely none. This is a conversation to see if regenerative medicine makes sense for your practice. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you honestly.

How long is the consultation?

Typically 30-45 minutes, depending on your questions and practice complexity.

Will I be pressured to buy products?

No. Our goal is to educate and help you make an informed decision. Many providers take weeks or months after the initial call to implement.

What if I'm brand new to regenerative medicine?

Perfect. We'll start with the basics and build from there. No prior knowledge needed.

Can I bring my business partner or practice manager?

Absolutely. The more key decision-makers involved, the better.
